The Head of Department leads all manufacturing and engineering activities and ensures that work goals are achieved on time and within budget. He/She comes up with business plans to solve problems by finding, developing and using new manufacturing methods. He/She also promotes the goals of the organisation. He/She is responsible for organisational excellence. He/She develops business solutions by designing and suggesting plans and programmes to boost the organisation's competitive position and increase profits. The Head of Department takes care of all matters relating to the staff of the department. He/She is responsible for managing staff performance. He/She builds teams that work well together and are able to carry out plans.
